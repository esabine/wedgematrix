Understanding Percentiles

You cannot choose which percentile you hit. You swing your best every time. The ball lands where it lands. Percentiles describe the natural spread in your past shots.

Picture your shots sorted shortest to longest. P50 is the middle one. P75 sits further up. P90 is near the top. The higher the percentile, the further up the sorted list you go. That is why the distances increase.

Think of it like a caddie telling you: "You usually carry 150. Three out of four times you reach at least 155." Pick the number that fits the situation. That is the whole idea.
