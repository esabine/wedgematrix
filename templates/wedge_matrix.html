Understanding Percentiles

You swing the same way for each clock position. The ball just does not always go the same distance. Percentiles capture that natural spread so you can plan smarter.

Sort your past shots for a swing size, shortest to longest. P50 is the midpoint. P75 is further up. P90 is near the top. Higher percentile means picking from further up the sorted list. That is why the carry number goes up.

Pin at 85? Check your SW 2/3 at P75. If it reads 83, that is your play. If it reads 78, bump up to 3/3.
