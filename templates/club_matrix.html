What Do Percentiles Mean?

A percentile tells you the distance you can reliably count on. If your 7-iron carry is P75 = 155 yards, that means 75% of your shots with that club carried at least 155 yards — it's your "dependable" number for course management.

Lower percentiles (P25, P50) are conservative picks — use them when there's trouble short of the green. Higher percentiles (P90, P95) reflect your best strikes — useful when you need to carry a bunker or reach a long par 5.

Example: With P75 selected and a 9-iron carry of 130 yards, you know 3 out of 4 shots will fly at least 130. That's a solid club selection number.
